Is GradMatch really free?

Yes! There are no hidden fees. Employers pay us to find great candidates like you, so you never have to pay to access our job listings or features.

Nope! We specialize in entry-level and internship roles designed specifically for recent graduates with little to no professional experience. We help you highlight your academic projects, coursework, and transferable skills.

Most graduates receive 5+ tailored job matches within 24 hours of completing their profile. We send new matches every weekday based on your preferences.
